# Project Requirements

## Identified Problem or Need

There is a problem that many people who regualarly organise music rosters, such as my church, where it is a lengthy process, where they have to send out many emails, and get feedback on availavility. Then, they have to manually go through on excell, and enter all the dates and people. This can take multiple iterations to make sure everyone is happy, and is available on the weeks they are rostered on for. It is a very lengthy process that could be shortened and simplified with a well implemented web app.


## End-User Requirements

There are two main end users. Admin Type end users, and musician end users. For admin end users, they would want the app to run on their laptop, as that is what they will want to use to create and edit the roster. They will want the system to clearly display the current roster, remind them when a new roster needs to be made, and have an easy interface to edit the roster, as they may be a bit older than the average user. The musician end users will want to be able to use this app on their phones and computers, as phones may be more convinent, but they might need to look quickly if they are at work, an if they use a computer at work, this would be faster. They will want to be able to easily input unavailabilities.


## Proposed Solution

I will solve this problem by creating an app that aids creating a roster. This app will allow musicians to log in, and select dates they are unavailable, what instrument they play, and such. They will also be able to request changes at any time if anything comes up, and see what weeks they are on. It will also allow an admin to see all this data, and easily create a roster on the app, and easily edit the roster.


# Relevant Implications

## Accessibility

The implication of accessibility refers to how accessible the app is to all people on all devices, such as: Does the app work well on phones, tablets, laptops and desktops?, Does the app contain semantic tags, and alt text on images to help people with screen readers?, and Is the app able to be easily used by people who have certain disabilities or people of all age groups?

### Relevance to the System

This implication is relevant to this project, as I will need this app to be used on both mobile phones and desktops/laptops, which relates to the device type aspect of accessibility. I will also need to make sure the app is easily accessible by older people, which relates to the accessibility of the site to older people.

### Impact / Considerations

When I make this app, I will need to consider design choices that will make my app accessible on both computers and phones, as if I make the design only compatable for one screen type, people who use the other may have a very hard time navigating the app, so it would not be accessible to them. I also need to make considerations that make it easy for older people or other people who are not as good at using technology, as if I do not, I might end up making an app that is very to complicated for these people to use, so it would not be accessible to them.



## Useability

Useability refers to how easy the app is to use. For example, Does the app help users minimise and undo errors?, Does the app follow Nielsens Usability Heuristics?, Does the app follow common conventions to maximise user useability?

### Relevance to the System

This implication is relevant to this project, as the app has to be useable by the end users for it to be successful. I am going to make an app that needs to be able to be used by the users, else they will not use it as it won't make their life easier, and will not use my app.

### Impact / Considerations

When I make my app, I will need to consider Nielsens Useability Heuristics while designing the layout and UX of the app, so the users have a good time using my app. This implication will impact my app, as it will severely impact my design, as I will need to make sure my app is useable when I design the app, and when I implement my design.

Functionality refers to how functional the app is, in terms of things like glitches and bugs, loading times, working on all browsers and devices, and links working as expected.
